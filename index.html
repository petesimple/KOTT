Quick vibe: King of the Table is pickup air hockey where the table is the throne and the queue is the drama.

  1. Lineup 3 active players: red side, yellow side, and the green waiting puck. Everyone else chills in the rotation line.
  2. Winner stays Tap the puck that scored. Scorer stays on. Loser rotates out. Waiting player jumps in. Extra humans go to the back of the line.
  3. Scoring to win First to 7 points or first to collect 3 blue shells wins the throne for this game.
  4. Blue shell chaos If you have 6 and get scored on, your score drops to 0 and the shooter earns 1 blue shell. Little shell icons show up next to their name.
  5. Buttons in plain English Swap Sides: trade ends before the first point. After someone scores, you are locked in, no backsies. Restart Game: same players, scores and shells back to 0, timer reset. Reset Game: nuke it all and start fresh with new names. Undo Last: fix a fat finger if someone tapped the wrong puck. Time Out: 10 second breather. If you still get scored on, that is between you and the air hockey gods. Add / Remove Player: manage the chaos in the queue.
  6. House spirit rule If you find a real bug, tell Pete. You earn a bug bounty crown and at least one imaginary blue shell in your heart.
